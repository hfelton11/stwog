
== Description ==
:This template is used to create a six-gem infoicon for a character in their infoboxes.  It is used to delineate the ordering of the diplomacy-values section of the infobox for a character.

;Syntax
:<code>{{t|GemSix|six initials for colors, in Left-to-Right order}}</code>
