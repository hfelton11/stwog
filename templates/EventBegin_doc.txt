;Description
:This template is used to for a quick introductory section in the [[:Star Trek Wrath of Gems Wikia:Calendar]] for a new Event.  It is the first of 3 templates used for [[:Category:Events]] - EventBegin, EventMid, and EventEnd. It can be called-directly, or called as a B-parameter for the template:Event.  It assumes that the details of the Event itself will be fully described at {{PAGENAME}}.

;Syntax
<pre>
{{EventBegin
 | (1)  = Name, defaults to PAGENAME
 | (2)  = Type-(O/N), defaults to the string <TOS or TNG>
 | (3)  = Description, defaults to <???>
}}
</pre>

;Sample(s)
:<pre>{{EventBegin| Empty of the Mind | O | the Entellans }}</pre>

Results in...

{{EventBegin| Empty of the Mind | O | the Entellans }}
