;Description
:This template is used for a quick introductory paragraph on the page describing an  Event.  It is one of 4 templates used for [[:Category:Events]] - EventBegin, EventMid, and EventEnd are for the [[:Star Trek Wrath of Gems Wikia:Calendar|calendar]]; while EventIntro is used on the actual Event-page, itself. It can be called-directly, or called as an I-parameter for the template:Event.  It assumes that the details of the Event itself will be fully described at {{PAGENAME}}.

;Syntax
<pre>
{{EventIntro
 | (1)  = Name, defaults to PAGENAME
 | (2)  = Type-(O/N), defaults to the string <TOS or TNG>
 | (3)  = Description, defaults to <???>
}}
</pre>

;Sample(s)
:<pre>{{EventIntro| Empty of the Mind | O | the Entellans }}</pre>

Results in...

{{EventIntro| Empty of the Mind | O | the Entellans }}
